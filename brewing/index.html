« Gathering the Required Supplies

You can't brew beer without the proper equipment and ingredients! Fortunately, getting what you need is much easier nowadays. I would recommend checking out your local brew supply store or the Internet for home brew starter kits. They will have almost everything you need to get started. The only thing I had to buy separately was a pot big enough to boil the ingredients in as well as a racking cane (nice to have, but not necessary). Of course, you will have to buy bottles separately (or collect empties as you drink store bought beer) and the ingredients. However, you can also buy ingredient kits. The table below gives you an itemized list of supplies you will need. Since this is an introduction to beer brewing, we will discuss beer brewing using an ingredient kit.
